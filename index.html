01 — The Big Picture

ICT Is Now a Quarter of Malaysia's Economy

Malaysia's digital economy has grown from just 18.5% of GDP in 2019 to 23.4% in 2024. This rapid rise — driven by both the ICT industry and booming e-commerce — places Malaysia among the most digitally advanced economies in Southeast Asia.

03 — E-Commerce Boom

How Malaysians Buy and Sell Online

E-commerce income in Malaysia reached RM1.18 trillion in 2023, growing at 16% annually since 2015. Business-to-business transactions dominate, but consumer e-commerce is the fastest-growing segment, reshaping how everyday Malaysians shop and transact.
